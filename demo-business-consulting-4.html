Production Process

From Vision to Reality: Crafting
Excellence in Every Stitch

1

Order Placement & Consultation

At this stage, customers place their orders and consult with our team regarding their production needs, including material selection, design, and quantity . Our team provides expert recommendations based on the customer's specifications. Once everything is finalized, an initial cost estimation is calculated based on the chosen materials and production complexity.

2

Costing & Timeline

After confirming the order details, our team creates patterns according to the design requirements. We check material availability and establish a production timeline to ensure a smooth workflow. Simultaneously, raw materials are procured from trusted suppliers to guarantee that all production needs are met on schedule.

3

Production Preparation

The production process starts with fabric cutting, stitching, and assembling garments according to the approved design. After production, the garments undergo a washing process to enhance durability and comfort. The finishing stage includes ironing, labeling, and final touches. Every finished product undergoes a strict Quality Control process before packaging to ensure it meets our high standards.

4

Manufacturing & Quality Control

The production process starts with fabric cutting, stitching, and assembling garments according to the approved design. After production, the garments undergo a >washing process to enhance durability and comfort. The finishing stage includes ironing, labeling, and final touches. Every finished product undergoes a strict Quality Control process before packaging to ensure it meets our high standards.

5

Packing & Delivery

Once the products pass Quality Control, they are carefully packed to maintain quality during transportation. After the packaging process , the orders are shipped to customers on time , ensuring trust and satisfaction with our services.
